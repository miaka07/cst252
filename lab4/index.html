Challenge

For this lab we have to work with a partner to get into the idea of pseudocoding. We are creating a list of high-level tasks and then breaking them down into sub-steps.

Probelms

We did not have any difficulties with this lab.

Results

Here are the results of our lab.
List for Changing a tire:
1. Open the trunk
2. Take out the jack & spare tire
3. Take off the flat tire
4. Put on the spare tire
5. Lower the jack
6. Replace the jack & put tire in trunk
7. Close the trunk
